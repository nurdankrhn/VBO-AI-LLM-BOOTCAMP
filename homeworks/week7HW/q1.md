# Highest Completed-Order Revenue by Month

## Result
**Month:** 2023-11  
**Revenue:** 245728.354

## Explanation
This analysis is based on completed orders only (status == "completed"). Revenue was calculated for each order line using the formula: unit_price × quantity × (1 - discount_pct / 100). The results were grouped by calendar month in YYYY-MM format and summed. November 2023 had the highest completed-order revenue at $245,728.35.
