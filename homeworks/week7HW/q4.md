# Top 3 Countries by Completed-Order Revenue

## Rankings

1. **US**: $1,008,048.95
2. **DE**: $452,360.71
3. **UK**: $444,536.92

## Methodology

This analysis is based on completed orders only (status == "completed"). Revenue for each order line was calculated using the formula: `unit_price * quantity * (1 - discount_pct / 100)`. The results were grouped by country and summed to determine total completed-order revenue per country.
