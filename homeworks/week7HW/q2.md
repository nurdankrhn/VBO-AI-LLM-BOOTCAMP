# Category with Most Completed-Order Revenue

**Category:** Electronics

**Revenue:** $2,112,140.5985

## Explanation

This analysis filtered the orders dataset to include only orders with status "completed" and calculated revenue for each line item using the formula: `unit_price * quantity * (1 - discount_pct / 100)`. The revenue was then grouped by category and summed. Electronics generated the highest completed-order revenue at $2,112,140.5985, significantly outperforming other categories (Home: $449,410.70, Clothing: $241,096.80, Toys: $79,096.15, Books: $51,187.05).
