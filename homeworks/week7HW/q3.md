## Trend Analysis

**Strong positive correlation exists**: Higher discount percentages are strongly associated with higher refund/cancellation rates. Orders with 0% discount have a 4.49% refund/cancellation rate, while orders with 40% discount have a 30.34% rate—nearly 7 times higher. This suggests that customers who receive larger discounts are significantly more likely to refund or cancel their orders.
